We live in a world full of answers. I'm more interested in the questions.

This is where I keep ideas that won't leave me alone, observations that don't fit anywhere else, and conversations I'd like to have more often. Some are about work. Most aren't.

I have a feeling there are a lot of people carrying around thoughts they never get to finish — not because they aren't worth sharing, but because they never found the right audience.

Consider this a place for those things. If you have a question you can't shake — send it my way. If it's interesting enough, we'll pull on the thread together.

Work with me

ways I can help

Conversations
—
Business Consulting

The obvious problem is rarely the real problem.

Most leaders already know what isn't working. What's harder is understanding why. That's where a different perspective can change everything.

If you're running a business and something feels stuck — growth, revenue, positioning, brand, funnels, operations, or execution — I'd be happy to take a look.

I work best on problems that have already been thought about seriously. If you've been sitting with a challenge for a while and want a fresh pair of eyes, that's usually a good place to start.

Student Consulting

Big decisions deserve better thinking.

Choosing a university. Applying abroad. MBA applications. Career pivots. Starting over.

I don't believe in generic advice. I help people think through decisions that will matter long after the offer letter arrives.

Product

Have a vision? Let's turn it into reality.

Most products fail not because they're built badly, but because they're built for the wrong reasons. I help early-stage teams go from idea to MVP and pressure-test whether the market cares before you've spent the budget finding out it doesn't.

People buy for their reasons, not yours. That's the whole job.

Pro Bono

There's no right time to give back. This is just my way of doing it.

If you're an NGO, nonprofit, government school, educator, or someone working on something that genuinely matters but doesn't have the budget for consulting — reach out anyway.

I'd rather spend an afternoon on something worthwhile than pass on it for a reason that won't matter a year from now.

If any of this sounds relevant, email is the right place to start. A short note about what you're working on is enough.

I'm Shradha. Most people call me Shra.

Original

Education is your asset. Knowledge is the one superpower nobody can take from you. I've built my career around that belief — and spent seven years in education making sure more people get access to both.

Education is the one thing that compounds quietly. You don't always feel it working. But somewhere between a book you read at 22 and a conversation you had at 35, something shifted.

I take on consulting when the problem is interesting enough. I'm selective — not because I'm oversubscribed, but because bad fits waste everyone's time.

Outside work: I travel when I can, read more than I finish, and pick up new skills like they're on discount. Currently learning French. Also can solve Hidato and Word puzzles in under a minute — impressive or a warning sign, depending on how you look at it.

Puzzle person. Art enthusiast. Still working on the meaning of life.

Life's a game — show up to win, stay to learn.
